Exchanges

How Exchanges Work

We offer exchanges for different fragrances or sizes. The process is similar to returns - initiate an exchange request, return the original product, and we'll ship your new choice once we receive the return.

Exchange Fees

No additional fees for exchanges. We cover return shipping for US customers, and you only pay the price difference if the new item costs more.

International Exchanges

International customers are responsible for return shipping costs. We recommend using a trackable shipping service and purchasing shipping insurance.

Refund Information

Processing Time

Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after we receive and inspect your return.

Payment Method

Refunds are issued to the original payment method. Credit card refunds may take 1-2 billing cycles to appear.

Shipping Costs

Original shipping costs are non-refundable, unless the return is due to our error.

Need Help with a Return?

Our customer service team is here to help you with any return or exchange questions.
