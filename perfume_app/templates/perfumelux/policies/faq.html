Products

How long does perfume last?

Most perfumes last 3-5 years if stored properly in a cool, dark place away from direct sunlight and extreme temperatures. Citrus-based fragrances may have a shorter shelf life of 2-3 years.

What's the difference between Eau de Parfum and Eau de Toilette?

Eau de Parfum has a higher concentration of fragrance oils (15-20%) and lasts longer on the skin (6-8 hours). Eau de Toilette has a lower concentration (5-15%) and typically lasts 3-4 hours. EDP is usually more expensive due to the higher concentration.

How should I store my fragrances?

Store fragrances in their original boxes in a cool, dark place away from direct sunlight, heat sources, and humidity. Avoid storing in bathrooms where temperature fluctuations can affect the fragrance.

Do you offer samples?

Yes! We offer sample sets and individual samples for most of our fragrances. Samples are a great way to test a scent before committing to a full bottle.

Ordering

How do I place an order?

Simply browse our collection, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout. You can checkout as a guest or create an account for faster future purchases.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover), PayPal, Apple Pay, and Google Pay.

Can I modify or cancel my order?

Orders can be modified or cancelled within 1 hour of placement. After that, they enter our processing system and cannot be changed. Please contact us immediately if you need to make changes.

Shipping

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping takes 3-5 business days within the US. Express shipping takes 2-3 business days, and next-day delivery is available for orders placed before 2 PM.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship to over 50 countries worldwide. International shipping times vary from 7-21 business days depending on the destination.

How is my order packaged?

All orders are carefully packaged in protective boxes with bubble wrap to prevent damage during transit. Fragile items receive extra protection.

Returns & Exchanges

What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 30 days of delivery for unused, unopened products in their original packaging. Used fragrances cannot be returned for health and safety reasons.

How do I initiate a return?

Contact our customer service team or use your account portal to initiate a return. We'll provide you with a return label and instructions.

How long do refunds take?

Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after we receive your return. Credit card refunds may take 1-2 billing cycles to appear on your statement.
